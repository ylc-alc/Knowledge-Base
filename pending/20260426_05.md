# Knowledge Summary
Ten specific prompt strategies to turn NotebookLM into a powerful research assistant beyond simple summarization.

# Extracted Details
- **Topic Scanning**: Scan for knowledge gaps before asking deep questions.
- **Conflict Detection**: Compare multiple sources to find contradictions and evaluate evidence strength.
- **Cross-Audience Briefing**: Repackage the same research for different stakeholders (executives vs. investors).
- **Blind Spot Discovery**: Force the AI to find insights you might have missed.
- **Timeline Reconstruction**: Rebuild causal links between key events.
- **Counter-Argument Defense**: Ask AI to find weaknesses in your thesis before publication.

# Key Takeaways
- NotebookLM is most effective when used as a dialectic partner, not just a summarizing tool.
- The 'Deep Research' feature helps in synthesizing complex information from dozens of papers.

# Actionable Tips
- Try the 'Conflict Detection' prompt to compare two opposing viewpoints in your source material.
