# Knowledge Summary
Introduction to 'nomadtable', a social app designed specifically for solo travelers. It focuses on activity-based meetups (eating, coffee, walking) to reduce the psychological pressure of formal social "matching" [cite: 3].

# Extracted Details
- **Core Purpose**: Facilitates finding companions for specific activities during solo travel, such as eating, drinking coffee, city walks, or working together [cite: 3].
- **User Workflow**: Users download the app, view nearby activities, chat briefly about details, and then meet up [cite: 3].
- **Design Philosophy**: Reduces social anxiety by focusing on the "action/activity" rather than the pressure of "making new friends" [cite: 3].
- **Growth Statistics**: Founded in late 2024 with a team of fewer than five people; has achieved over 700,000 global users [cite: 3].
- **Demographics**: Currently dominated by Western travelers, though local hosting is increasing in cities like Taipei [cite: 3].

# Key Takeaways
- The app's success is attributed to "breaking social interaction into low-cost psychological actions" [cite: 3].
- Rapid growth (700k+ users) with a tiny team indicates a strong product-market fit for solo travel solutions [cite: 3].
