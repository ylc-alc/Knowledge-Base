# Knowledge Summary
Ethan Mollick's experimental class at UPenn demonstrated how executive MBA students, mostly non-coders, could build working startups and prototypes in just four days using a suite of agentic AI tools.

# Extracted Details
- Class held at the University of Pennsylvania for executive MBA students.
- Tools used for technical prototyping: Claude Code and Google Antigravity.
- Business process acceleration: ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini were used for idea generation, market research, competitive positioning, pitching, and financial modeling.
- Result: Students reached advanced stages of startup development in an extremely short timeframe.

# Key Takeaways
- AI significantly lowers the barrier to entry for technical creation for non-technical leaders.
- The combination of management experience and AI tools acts as a significant force multiplier ('superpower').

# Actionable Tips
- Use multiple AI models (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) in parallel to handle different business verticals like research, finance, and coding.
