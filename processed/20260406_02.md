# Knowledge Summary
Google's March 2024 core update marks a major shift in how AI-generated content is ranked, with a focus on reducing low-quality 'spammy' content. The update also introduces tighter technical requirements and reflects a transition from traditional SEO to AI Engine Optimization (AEO).

# Extracted Details
- Google aims to reduce non-useful content by 40% through this update. [cite: 2]
- Websites relying purely on AI generation without human modification are experiencing drastic traffic declines. [cite: 2]
- Interaction to Next Paint (INP) requirements have tightened from 200ms to 150ms. [cite: 2]
- Traditional clicks have decreased by 42% due to AI Overviews, while Google Discover clicks have increased by 30%. [cite: 2]
- The SEO paradigm is shifting from 'fighting for #1' to 'becoming a source for AI citations.' [cite: 2]

# Key Takeaways
- - Real-world experience and unique perspectives are the most valuable traffic 'passwords' in the AI era. [cite: 2]
