# Knowledge Summary
This note captures a 10-step strategy shared by 'me4.0.version' based on Alec Hormozi's video on how to leverage AI for competitive advantage in 2026. The focus is on moving from fear to active integration, treating AI as a high-speed employee and force multiplier.

# Extracted Details
- **Work Deconstruction**: Don't just replace workers; deconstruct jobs into tasks where AI can automate specific high-efficiency parts.
- **Training AI**: AI doesn't get tired or quit. Provide clear rules and examples (few-shot prompting) to see rapid improvement.
- **AI as a Multiplier**: One person using AI can generate the value previously created by a whole team.
- **Finding the Balance**: Balance AI-driven efficiency with human-driven unique value and emotional resonance.
- **Bias Toward Action**: Overcome anxiety by starting immediately.
- **New Tools, New Thinking**: Don't apply old linear workflows to new AI capabilities; rethink the process.
- **The Power of the Question**: Prompting and "asking the right questions" is the most important skill in the AI era.
- **Embrace Imperfect Drafts**: Use AI to jump from 0 to 60% completion instantly, then spend human effort moving from 60% to 90%.
- **Focus on ROI**: Automate repetitive tasks to free up time for high-leverage decision-making.
- **Continuous Iteration**: The field moves fast; staying curious and iterative is the only way to avoid being replaced.

# Key Takeaways
- AI is not a replacement for humans but a tool for those who can redesign their work around it.
- Speed of learning and execution is the primary competitive advantage.

# Actionable Tips
- Identify one repetitive task today and try to get AI to do the first 60% of it.
- Practice "asking the right questions" by refining prompts based on logical structures rather than just keywords.
