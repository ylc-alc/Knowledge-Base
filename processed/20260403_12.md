Summary of Dario Amodei's January 2026 essay, "The Adolescence of Technology." The piece uses a metaphor from Carl Sagan's *Contact* to discuss how humanity must evolve and survive the risks associated with powerful AI [cite: 2].

- **Focus**: Confronting and overcoming the risks of powerful AI [cite: 2].
- **Context**: Published in early 2026 by the CEO of Anthropic [cite: 2].
