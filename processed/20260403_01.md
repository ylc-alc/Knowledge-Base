# Knowledge Summary
This note summarizes a skill demand framework for 2030 based on data from the World Economic Forum (WEF) "Future of Jobs Survey 2024." It categorizes skills into four quadrants based on their current importance (2025) versus their projected growth in demand by 2030.

# Extracted Details
The framework identifies four categories of skills:

- **2030 Core Skills (High Importance, High Growth):**
  - AI and Big Data
  - Technological Literacy
  - Creative Thinking
  - Resilience, Flexibility, and Agility
  - Analytical Thinking
  - Curiosity and Lifelong Learning
  - Talent Management

- **Emerging Skills (Low Current Importance, High Growth):**
  - Networks and Cybersecurity
  - Environmental Stewardship
  - Design and User Experience

- **Steady Skills (High Importance, Low Growth):**
  - Motivation and Self-awareness
  - Empathy and Active Listening
  - Leadership and Social Influence
  - Service Orientation and Customer Service
  - Resource Management and Operations
  - Dependability and Attention to Detail

- **Out of Focus Skills (Low Importance, Low Growth):**
  - Programming (Note: showing low relative growth/importance in this specific context)
  - Multi-lingualism
  - Global Citizenship
  - Reading, Writing, and Mathematics
  - Sensory-processing Abilities
  - Manual Dexterity, Endurance, and Precision

# Key Takeaways
- **AI Dominance:** AI and Big Data are positioned as the most critical growth areas for the next decade.
- **Human-Centric Value:** Soft skills like "Creative Thinking" and "Resilience" are considered core for 2030, showing that human-centric capabilities remain vital alongside technical shifts.
- **The Shift in Technical Skills:** Interestingly, basic "Programming" is classified in a lower-growth quadrant, potentially reflecting the rise of low-code/no-code or AI-assisted coding tools.

# Actionable Tips
- **Prioritize Literacy:** Focus on "Technological Literacy" as a baseline requirement for all roles.
- **Invest in Soft Skills:** Cultivate "Resilience" and "Lifelong Learning" to navigate the rapid shifts in technological requirements.
- **AI Integration:** Actively seek ways to integrate AI and data analysis into current workflows to stay ahead of the growth curve.
