# Knowledge Summary
Stanford University launched STORM, a free AI research tool designed for deep topic exploration rather than standard conversational chat.

# Extracted Details
- Free and accessible to everyone.
- Focuses on research tasks rather than conversational interaction.
- Input a topic (e.g., 'Pros and cons of TikTok marketing') to generate a comprehensive report.
- Output structure: Summary, Pros & Cons, Best Practices, Case Study, and Key Takeaways.
- Key feature: Cites multiple sources to provide a diverse range of viewpoints.
- Current limitation: Only supports English language input.
- URL: storm.genie.stanford.edu

# Key Takeaways
- Specialized research AI tools are diverging from general-purpose LLMs by prioritizing structured, multi-source synthesis.

# Actionable Tips
- Use STORM for initial deep-dives into complex topics to get a balanced overview with citations.
