# Knowledge Summary
A structured sequence of three AI prompts designed to assist researchers in identifying core theories, gathering high-quality references, and managing citations.

# Extracted Details
- Concept Extraction: Prompt to identify 5-8 core concepts and representative scholars for a topic.
- Literature Generation: Prompt to find 15-20 high-quality SSCI/SCIE references with DOIs within the last 10 years.
- BibTeX Conversion: Instruction to transform the identified reference list into BibTeX format.

# Actionable Tips
- Use the first prompt to validate research directions before deep diving.
- Ensure DOIs are requested to verify the existence of the generated references.
