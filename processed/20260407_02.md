# Knowledge Summary
Infographic outlining the evolution of search visibility from traditional SEO to AI-driven Answer, Generative, and Integration Optimization.

# Extracted Details
- SEO: Focuses on ranking through keywords and domain authority. Basis of visibility.
- AEO: Aimed at winning 'Position Zero' (featured snippets) and appearing in voice/instant results.
- GEO: Involves creating 'quotable content' and adding structured schema for LLMs like Gemini or ChatGPT to cite.
- AIO: Focuses on getting content into training data and AI-powered workflows like shopping assistants or CRMs.

# Key Takeaways
- Traditional SEO is the foundation, but GEO and AEO are necessary for AI-powered visibility.
- Without GEO, AI platforms cannot effectively parse or reference your content.
# Actionable Tips
- Implement structured schema to help GEO engines.
- Optimize for direct, clear answers to capture AEO traffic.
